Depression Detection based on Audio and Text Data

A coursework on Audio and text based Depression Classification using fused model (LSTM+GRU). The experiment also explored on how data enlarging and resampling affect the result.

Video based Speed Measurement

The project focused on vehicle speed estimation based on videos and was therefore built based on object recognition and tracking algorithm. A calibration method is also implemented here to fix the tortion of distance caused by Perspective. Detailed code will be released with paper later.

OCR Detection Paper Review

OCR is becoming more and more frequently used in our daily lives and is also developing rapidly in recent years. Click in and see a review on recent papers.

Safe Logistics: Django based web database inquiry system

Group project that focus on practicing our skills on SQL and Django. The project aims to provide information for sellers, buyers and logistics companies. And it also provides info on domestic pandemic (covid-19) situation so that buyers can be notified on potential epidemic risk.

Analysis of Vaccination on Markovian Random Walk Model of epidemic proliferation

The project simulated the spread of the epidemic under SIR model by using Markovian random walk and the paper then discussed the impact of epidemic prevention policies and vaccines on the spread of the epidemic.
